Edinburgh airport is one of the best to spot at in Scotland. Offering a range of areas including a well used mound, and a variety of cariers, EDI/EGPH is well used. Here are my favourite spots;

Note; Spotting/taking pictures at any of these spots is completley fine, just do not shake/touch any of the permitre fences.

Spot A - Inglston - Mound

Located parallel to Alpha link, the mound is the ideal place to spot. It is realitivy high - fences are not a problem when shooting from here - Here are some shots taken from the mound;

 

Perosnally, I only enjoy to use the mound when runway 06 is in use, this means that aircraft are on minimums 50 ft from you, and they taxi right by you.

 

Spot B - Almondbank

 

Located opposite the terminal building, Almondbank offers a nice setting for some photography. Similiar to the farm road at GLA you will be shooting through a fence.

Spot C - Bravo Link (Inglston)

 

A couple hundred yards down from the mound, Bravo link at Inglston offers some nice turnoff, and rotation shots when 24 is in use.
