Glasgow airport is the 2nd bussiest in Scotland. But GLA has more trans-atlantic/international flights to its rival Edinburgh, so fi you like bigger planes, this is the place to go. Destinations include; Orlando, Halifax, Toronto, Vancuver, Dubai, Phillidelphia, NewYork, Cancun, and many more. Here are my selected spots;

 

Spot A - Farm Road -

This was the first place I spotted at. The farmroad (Walkinshaw Road) is similar to Almondbank at EDI, with a full veiw of the airport, and cracking photo opertunities with both runways!
